This is where I think out loud.

I spend my days getting AI agents out of demos and into production, and a lot of what I learn never gets written down. The fix that took three days. The eval that finally caught a regression. The deployment that looked done but wasn't. So I'm keeping notes here, mostly for me, and partly for anyone doing the same kind of work.

No theme, no schedule. Short notes when something is worth keeping.

A few things I want to get into:

- What actually breaks when you put agents in front of real users
- Evals that are worth the effort, and the ones that aren't
- The boring infrastructure that makes a demo hold up under load

If any of it is useful to you, good. If you want to argue with it, even better. The links up top are the best way to reach me.

From demo to deployed.
